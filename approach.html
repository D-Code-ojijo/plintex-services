Benefits of attending this course

At the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  1. Monitor and control debt levels leading to better cash flow.
  2. Relate debt management to company profitability.
  3. Handle and overcome challenges experienced in the process of debt collection.
  4. Sell benefits of payment rather than the consequences of non-payment.
  5. Confidently conduct collection calls against any debtor characteristic.
  6. Negotiate with customers to pay without demanding for payment.
  7. Improve customer relationships for improved business.
  8. Improve on the collections
  9. Gain a deeper understanding of the debt/receivables purchase industry.

These learning experiences have been expressly selected to validate, complement, support, and reinforce your existing and developing training programs, concentrating on that individual who must be the critical epicenter of your organization—the Customer.
