Overview

The management of risk lies at the heart of every business. One of the main risks financial and commercial institutions incur arises from extending credit to debtors through their lending and trading operations.

Effective risk management is fundamental to being able to generate profits consistently and sustainably and is thus a central part of the financial and operational management of every institution.

Through their risk management framework, financial and commercial institutions manage enterprise-wide risks, with the objective of maximizing risk-adjusted returns while remaining within their risk appetite.

However, the most profound approach is to equip the team with extensive in-depth skills in credit, risk and debt management that allows for constant and continuous revamp of strategies and tactics that will keep up with the ever changing responses/excuses that our customers keep building – professional training.
